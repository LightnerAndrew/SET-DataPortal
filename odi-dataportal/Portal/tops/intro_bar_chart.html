Bar Chart - Country Comparison

This interactive plot compares changes in economic transformation indicators over time across countries. Choose the country comparison selection by holding Ctrl and clicking on the state of interest. There are three options when visulising time-series data: the user can average all years chosen (Averages), visualise the time pattern by country (Country/Year), or examine each country at a given period of time (Year/Country). Within the latter two plots, users can average subgroups of years by using 'Year Groupings' option.
