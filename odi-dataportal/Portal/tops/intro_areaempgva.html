Structural Change: Employment and Gross Value Added Proportions

The process of economic transformation composes of two related but distinct processes. One component is structural change, whereby the share of the labour force employed in relatively high-productivity sectors increases. This process has often been related to the reduction in the share of the labour force in agriculture and an increasing share of employment in manufacturing and high-productivity services. Structural changes in share of gross value added typically track these changes in labour force activities.
